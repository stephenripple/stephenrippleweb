About Me

I have been interested in technology ever since I learned the potential to harness the awesome power of computers when I was around 8 years old. A very simple BASIC program that instructed the computer to alter screen colors opened my mind to an infinite number of possibilities that computers would help to make reality. In my free time I enjoy developing and playing video games, game console modifications, home automation, tinkering with Raspberry PI and learning emerging technologies.

I am currently learning about container applications and tools around continuous deployment/delivery and automation while heavily leveraging cloud based platforms.

When I have the time, I try to take an international trip to try and learn about different cultures. So far, I have been to: Spain, Germany, Switzerland, Japan, Peru, Bolivia and Netherlands.
